about diabetes

Diabetes in a nutshell is an issue with your body's ability to produce insulin. This condition is medically dubbed "hyperglycemia". There are sevreal different types of diabetes.

1

Type 1 Diabetes

5 % of people are diagnosed with Type 1 diabetes. Usually diagnosedi in children and young adults. In type 1 diabetes, no, or very little, insulin is realsed into the body.

2

Type 2 Diabetes

The most common form of diabetes, Type 2 consists of the body failing to properly use insulin, an ailment most commonly refered to as insulin resistance.

G

Gestional Diabetes

Temporary condition that occurs during pregnancy. It affects approximately two to four per cent of all pregnancies. Involves an increased risk of developing diabetes for both mother and child.
